4 steps to win

*20% of the game stack we send to charities in the Games area. 50% of the stack is burned away at no cost.

  • Be at the top of the single player list to be able to join the hunger games.
  • Complete a series of quests. In each quest the weakest player is eliminated.
  • Get to the next-to-last stage. The last stage is open for the remaining players at the indicated time and location.
  • Be the first and win to receive 30% of the total stack of that particular hunger game.
