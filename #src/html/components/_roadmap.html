Perspective

2019-2020

An idea is concieved during the covid lockdown of an app that would allow people to more fully immerse in the outside world, augmented by cutting edge XR technology.

Q1 | 2023

onmi's core components and principles are founded, ideas and basic mechanics were drafted. The game began development.

Q2 | 2023

Key business decisions were made, development and content plan is established, key partners were contacted and collaboration efforts have started.

Q3 | 2023

onmi Whitepaper, product documentation, roadmap and other documents are finalized. Game Economy is fully calculated and a project team scaling strategy is developed.

Q4 | 2023

onmi is launched on Google Play and Apple App Store with complete core game mechanics. Internal tokenomics model is launched alongside the game. First clothing collections are launched are available for crafting and players start exchanging on the market.

Q1 | 2024

Omi Ghosts are launched, allowing newcomers to experience Reality 2.0 for free for a limited time. The hunger games perliminaries are carried out and the tournament is held worldwide. First competetive multilayer challenge is launched, based on a popular io game. ONM token is listed on DEX and CEX listings

Q2 | 2024

A new competitive challenge is launched: a collectible card game called "Napies". Cards in player inventories can now be utilized fully in this game mode. Competitive ranking is introduced, providing profit incentives for higher skilled players. onmi spots are released, encouraging players to hang out together.

Q3 | 2024

A new gameplay is added, where people join up and conquer territory block by block. A basic narrative is added to the game that expands on core principles and ideas of onmi. AR routes and festival utility is added, expanding Reality 2.0 more into the real world.

Q4 | 2024

onmi for creators programme is launched, allowing third parties to launch their custom content within the game. Teams are formed to create local game content and ARGs.

Q1 | 2025

onmi is released for upcoming wearable AR devices, allowing Reality 2.0 to transition into the new age. Orbs are no longer random. Players can submit their places using our specialized service to elevate interesting places and real-life art

Q2 | 2025

AR elements are expanded more: Collaborative activities like sculpting are added. Omi can now be interacted with in AR, clothes recieve further utility, allowing players to wear them in AR.

Q3 | 2025

Main storylines are established, ARGs are lauched. People are encouraged to explore real and virtual worlds to uncover clues and crucial story details.

Q4 | 2025

Omi + AI. Omi are expanded significantly- they are now living beings with emotions and will. Players can now talk to their Omi, discuss issues, find support and reflect on their past adventures. Omi will become intelligent and highly valued.
