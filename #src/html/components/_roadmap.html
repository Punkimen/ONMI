2019-2020

During the coronavirus pandemic, the creators devised an app allowing them to add another layer to reality while remaining fully engaged in what was happening around them. That is how ONMI was born.

2022

Pre-production and closed development

Throughout the year, we systematically developed ONMI’s core components.

Q3 | 2023

While the developers and game designers worked on creating an immersive virtual reality world, we established key business objectives.

We built relationships with customers as well as strategic partners.

Our team also developed an effective go-to-market strategy and worked on the product's architecture.

Q2 | 2023

Our developers finished the game concept and whitepaper, outlining the project’s details.

Another team created a working game economy and a human resource strategy.

ONMI’S technical infrastructure was based on microservices architecture and smart contracts.

We attracted investments using a lunch and privatization strategy. Then we released a testnet to present our work.

Q3 | 2023

The ONMI app was successfully launched on the App Store and Google Play Store, making it widely available to users. In addition, a convenient wallet was integrated into the app, allowing users to store and manage their assets and tokens.

The team also developed and implemented innovative AI-ML models, enhancing the unique ONMI user experience. We developed a well-defined strategy to attract Web2 users to the platform, increasing the user base and engagement.

Smart contracts were thoroughly audited to ensure user reliability and security, which helped build trust and protect assets. ONMI has been successfully floated on decentralized (DEX) and centralized (CEX) exchanges, providing liquidity and accessibility to token trading.

A mainnet was subsequently released, allowing users to experience ONMI’s full potential and functionality.

In addition, the team introduced the following gaming features:

Weather: Real-time weather effects on the in-game map that affect rewards.

Totems: Unique locations with totems that restore energy and eliminate wear and tear on the ONMI.

PvP Zones: Zones on the map designed for PvP battles

OMI Showcase: Users can place their OMI in various places on the map, showing them off to other users and farming likes.

Q4 | 2023

Clans: Unite with other players, create clans, and conquer resources together.

Conquer Regions: Fight for control of different areas on the map, gaining increased rewards from other players in these regions.

Conquer Clan Zones: Clans can establish and maintain dominance over certain regions, gaining exclusive benefits and rewards.

Boss Orbs: Engage in group battles against powerful boss orbs, earning significant rewards.

AR Objects in Cultural Landmarks: Find augmented reality objects associated with cultural landmarks, unlocking power-ups and hidden treasures.
