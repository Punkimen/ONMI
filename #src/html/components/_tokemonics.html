Tokenomics

ONMI has developed a scarcity-based economy where all valuable assets, such as tokens, resources and NFTs, have a limited supply.

Creating in-game clothing is a necessary step for players to level up their characters. If no one creates a certain item of clothing, it will not exist in the game.

As a result, players must use resources strategically to improve their characters and increase their chances of obtaining valuable materials, tokens, and models.
