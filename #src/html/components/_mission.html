Mission

To merge the real and digital worlds through the excitement of adventure and the thirst to achieve victories in treasure hunts. To help users relive familiar childhood sensations lost over time.

ONMI is a nexus-gen app because it seeks to rediscover the user's interaction with content and its author via narrative and game mechanics. The new society will have a combination of entertainment, content, and service apps, such as financial tools, chat rooms, and a marketplace.
